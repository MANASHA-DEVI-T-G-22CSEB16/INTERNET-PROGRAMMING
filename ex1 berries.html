Botanical Definition:
Berries are fruits produced from a single ovary and typically contain seeds embedded in the flesh.

Variety of Berries: There are numerous types of berries, including strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, blackberries, cranberries, and more.

Nutritional Value:
  • Berries are rich in essential nutrients, vitamins, and antioxidants.
  • They are particularly known for high levels of vitamin C, fiber, and various beneficial compounds.
Health Benefits: Regular consumption of berries is associated with health benefits, including improved heart health, reduced inflammation, and potential anti-cancer properties.

Antioxidants: Berries are packed with antioxidants such as anthocyanins, quercetin, and resveratrol, which help neutralize free radicals in the body.
